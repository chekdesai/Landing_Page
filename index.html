From snapping a photo to ranking your top trips, Rango handles the rest quietly in the background.

Smart Photo Scanning

Point your camera at any scene and Rango instantly identifies the location and context — automatically creating a rich log entry without you typing a word.

Trip Ranking System

Compare your adventures head-to-head. Rate trips across scenery, food, and culture, then let Rango build your personal leaderboard of the world's best experiences.

Journey Map

Watch your travel history come alive. Every logged trip pins itself to your personal world map, showing exactly how far your compass has pointed.

Travel Log & Timeline

Every trip becomes a beautifully organized entry in your journal — photos, locations, dates, and memories all sorted into a personal timeline you can revisit anytime.
